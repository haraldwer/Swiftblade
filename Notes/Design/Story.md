## Intro
The basic tutorial happens without the sword and takes the character to the central chamber. This only happens during the first playthrough. 
## The chamber
- Player reaches the central chamber
- Player picks up sword
	- Freeze player movement
	- Sword lights on fire
		- Fill room with light
- Cutscene
	- “The sword is mine”
		- Show eyes
	- Lighting, show the face of the wizard
	- “Only the worthy will escape”
		- Show hands
## Enter arena
- Sword soul goes in between doors
- Player reaches doors
	- Big entry, should feel epic!
- Player pushes doors open
	- Slow, heavy, creaking sound
	- Complete darkness within
- Player enters
	- Fades to black
- Fadein in arena
	- Enter arena from the side, do not drop down
	- Spawn enemies when inside arena
## Exit arena
- Player breaks crystal
	- Fill room with light
	- Fade to white
	- Fullscreen flames
- Player is teleported to next section
## Outro
- Option A: Player strikes the soul
	- Fill room with light
	- Fade to white
	- Show score screen
- Option B: Player jumps into a new space
	- Fade to white
	- Show score screen
