Low Poly. Medieval, macabre and grotesque. A bleak world with almost no color. There are exceptions, but primarily for gameplay purposes. 
- Rooms can use color to guide the player
- Objects that the player can interact with are highlighted
- Enemies are highlighted
## Colors

A triad of colors

Gameplay -  Turquoise

Fiender - Purple / Pink

Level - Grey with desaturated details
