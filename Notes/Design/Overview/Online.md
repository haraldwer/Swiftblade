The player can log in with a username. The player can link their username to an external account, such as steam. This username is now taken. 

Once the player has logged in, the player can view and compete on leaderboards. 
The leaderboards can be filtered in different ways: 

Social filter
- Global - Scores of all players
- Following - Scores of the players you follow
- Personal - Your personal scores

Seed filter
- Any - Scores of any seed
- Specific - Score of a specific seed
- Last played - Score of the last seed that you played

Chapter filter
- All - Score for all chapters
- Specific - Score for a specific chapter

The player can click on any score to bring up some options.
- Follow the player that set the score
- Play that specific seed

The player can view a list with the history of recent plays. 
If the player is replaying a seed, the player can see a ghost from their previous playthrough. 
Some leaderboard info is shown when completing a playthrough.
